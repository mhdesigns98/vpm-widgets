Meeting Families Where They Are

BilingualKid Language Immersion School in Glen Allen uses a fully embedded Spanish curriculum to help children develop bilingual and biliteracy skills by age 4. Claudia Robles was selected as a 2019 PBS Early Learning Champion.

▶

Community Story: Cargill

In partnership with Smart Beginnings Greater Harrisonburg, VPM shared The Basics Principles with employees of Cargill to encourage child development through everyday activities at home.

▶

Learning Neighborhoods Initiative

VPM partnered with ExCELL to create Learning Neighborhoods, bringing families, children, and educators together for interactive workshops at schools in Richmond and Petersburg.

▶

The Market at 25th Partnership

VPM partners with Richmond Public Schools and The Market at 25th to combine nutrition and food security with educational experiences, simplifying preschool enrollment for families in Richmond's East End.

▶

VPM Learning Wall & Little Free Library

Thanks to Elkton Family Pharmacy and Rockingham County Public Schools for establishing the VPM Learning Wall and Little Free Library, making educational resources and books available for families.

▶

Nature Play with Wildrock

Wildrock partners with VPM, Albemarle Garden Club, and Charlottesville Parks and Recreation to celebrate the benefits and importance of nature play for children and families.
